Zendesk Acting Operators

by Obi
June 2026

Anne is an operator for Zendesk. You connect with a token, she reads your workspace, and from there she handles real tickets for you: triaging the backlog, pulling up a customer's history, updating a status, leaving a note, closing things out. You tell her what you need in plain language and she takes care of it. The part we cared about most was keeping it safe. Some of what she can do, like issuing a refund or closing a ticket, is hard to undo, so anything risky runs through a verification gate that pauses and waits for you to confirm before it goes through. We're also training her with reinforcement learning to focus on what keeps customers around, and the model behind her can run in the cloud or on our own hardware. She's live today, so you can go try her.
